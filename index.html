POLICIES & CONSIDERATIONS

House Rules

To ensure a peaceful and hygienic stay experience for everyone, we request you to follow our house rules.

Smoking Policy Smoking is allowed, but guests must keep the premises clean and avoid disturbing others.
No Spitting or Littering Spitting and littering are strictly not allowed inside or around the homestay premises.
No Visitors Parties, large events, and unregistered outside visitors are not permitted.
Save Energy Please switch off the air conditioning and all electrical appliances when leaving rooms.
Quiet Hours

No loud music after 10:00 PM.

Please respect everyone's peace and sleep schedule after 10:00 PM. Keep conversation volumes reasonable in common areas.

Cleanliness & Kitchen Use dustbins and wash all separate kitchen utensils immediately after usage.
Do Not Move Furniture Please do not move or rearrange furniture, frames, or interior decor setups of the house.
Damage Policy Any material damage to the property will be charged extra to the guest.
